Do you need to know the rules of Tetris? Are you kidding me?

Different pieces are descending from the top until they touch the bottom of the field or land on a piece. In that case, they fill the space and create an obstacle to the new descending pieces. During the descend of the pieces, the player can move them. The player must move the pieces to form one or more horizontal lines made with blocks of pieces, to erase these lines and free that space occupied by the pieces. If the player cannot make the blocks disappear quickly enough, the field will start to fill, and when the pieces reach the top of the field and prevent the descend of additional pieces, the game ends.

Use the gamepad you find in the app to: rotate, move to the left or the right, or moving the piece down.

  • Left arrow: moves to the left
  • Right arrow: moves to the right
  • Down arrow: moves down
  • Up arrow: rotates 90 degrees counterclockwise

It's variation of the Snake game.

The player controls a round shape, a dot. The blue dot can move horizontally and vertically. At the beginning, the player has 20 lives. Goal of the game is to eat as many red dots as possible while staying alive. When the blue dot eats the target, he grows a tail and points are added to its score. When the blue dot hits one of the red obstacles, the player loses a life. The blue dot is then teleported to the centre but continues to move. When there are no more lives available, the game ends.

Use the gamepad you find in the app to: move to the left or the right, or going up or down. Instead of the gamepad, you can use the left, right, and down arrows on your keyboard.

  • Left arrow: moves to the left
  • Right arrow: moves to the right
  • Down arrow: moves down
  • Up arrow: moves up

This one is a simplified version of Twenty-one, also known as Blackjack.

The player must play against the dealer. Pressing 'hit' gives a random number (a number between 1 and 11). The player can continue to get numbers or stop. Each time the numbers are added to the total. When the player chooses 'stand' then it is time for the dealer to play. The winner is the one with the total closest to or equal to 21, without exceeding it.
